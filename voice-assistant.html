Conversation

Sample Q and A between a farmer and the Plantelligence assistant.

Hi Plantelligence, my tomato plants have yellowing leaves with brown spots. What could be the issue?
Based on your description, it may be early blight or a nutrient deficiency. Check whether older leaves are affected first and if the spots have dark concentric rings.
Yes, older leaves show circular dark spots, and the stems look weak. How should I treat it?
Remove severely affected leaves, ensure proper spacing for airflow, and apply a recommended fungicide as per label instructions. Avoid overhead irrigation if possible.
What can I do to prevent this in the next season?
Rotate crops, use disease-free seedlings, and add organic matter to improve soil health. Plantelligence can also monitor leaf health regularly once you scan images.
