Government Schemes

Official central schemes for India. Eligibility, components, and timelines vary by state or UT and are updated periodically, so use the official links for the latest details.

National schemes (India)

Each card summarizes the scheme and links to the official portal or info page.

PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

Direct income support of Rs 6,000 per year in three equal installments to eligible landholding farmer families via DBT.

Eligibility: Eligible landholding farmer families as per scheme guidelines (exclusions apply).
Official portal
PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)

Crop insurance that provides coverage and financial support when notified crops fail due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

Eligibility: Farmers growing notified crops in notified areas who enroll through the scheme.
Official portal
PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana)

Irrigation program to enhance on-farm water access, expand assured irrigation, and improve water-use efficiency through PMKSY components like Har Khet Ko Pani (HKKP).

Eligibility: Farmers and projects covered under state and district plans for PMKSY components.
Official portal
Soil Health Card Scheme

Soil Health Cards provide nutrient status of soil and recommended fertilizer or soil amendment doses to support balanced nutrient management.

Eligibility: Farmers covered by state or district soil testing schedules and SHC issuance cycles.
Official portal
e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)

Pan-India electronic trading portal that networks APMC mandis to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.

Eligibility: Farmers, traders, and FPOs in e-NAM linked mandis.
Official portal
Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

Financing facility to mobilize medium long-term debt for viable projects in post-harvest management infrastructure and community farming assets.

Eligibility: Eligible entities and projects as per AIF guidelines.
Official portal
SMAM (Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization)

Supports farm mechanization, including custom hiring centers and access to high-value equipment, especially for small and marginal farmers.

Eligibility: Farmers and groups as notified by state implementations.
Official portal
NFSM (National Food Security Mission)

Mission to increase production of rice, wheat, pulses, coarse cereals, and nutri-cereals through area expansion and productivity enhancement.

Eligibility: Farmers in NFSM-identified districts and components.
Official portal
Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Credit delivery mechanism to help farmers meet production credit requirements in a timely and hassle-free manner.

Eligibility: Farmers seeking crop production or allied activity credit through banks.
Official info
RKVY-RAFTAAR

Flexible, state-led program that incentivizes public investment in agriculture and allied sectors, with focus on watershed and rainfed systems.

Eligibility: Implemented through state plans; beneficiaries vary by state or UT.
Official portal
MIDH (Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture)

Centrally sponsored scheme for holistic growth of horticulture covering fruits, vegetables, spices, flowers, and more.

Eligibility: Farmers and groups covered under state horticulture action plans.
Official info
